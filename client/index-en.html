Rule Out

Assigns a high priority to patients with the following criteria:

intensiveCareInLastMonth true if the patient was admitted


cancer true patient with cancer diagnosis


immuneDefficiency true patient with immune defficiency diagnosis


dyspnea true if the patient has shortness of breath


acuteMotorImpairment true if the patient has any motor impairment or difficulty talking


chestPain true if the patient has chest pain


hematemesis true if the patient is vomiting blood


If the patient does not apply to any of these criteria it goes to the next scoring step
